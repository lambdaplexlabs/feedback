# Code of Conduct

Lambdaplex Labs is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free experience
for everyone participating in this repository -- whether you're reporting a bug,
requesting a feature, or joining a discussion.

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its Code of Conduct. Please read the full text there. By participating in this
community, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the Lambdaplex Labs team at **support@lambdaplex.io**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the
privacy and security of the reporter will be respected.
